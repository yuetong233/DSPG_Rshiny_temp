Welcome to the Household Energy App

This application demonstrates how raw household energy survey data is downloaded, cleaned, and analyzed. The interactive maps and charts allow you to explore household energy burdens and climate exposure across regions.

Data Pipeline

  1. 01_download.R – collects raw survey and climate data
  2. 02_clean.R – prepares tidy, standardized datasets
  3. 03_analysis.R – generates analysis outputs
  4. app.R – runs the interactive Shiny application
